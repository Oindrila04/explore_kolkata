Exploring the City of Joy

Kolkata is the third most populous metropolitan city after Delhi and Mumbai.It is known for its literary, artistic and revolutionary heritage; as the former capital of India, it was the birthplace of modern Indian literary and artistic thought and Kolkata has been called the "City of Furious, Creative Energy" as well as the "cultural capital of India".

Amidst the rapid globalization, there is a city which still preserves the pre- independence era; a city where the waves of the Ganges still sing of the glory of zamindars and the city where the houses preserve the age-old grandmother's bedtime stories.
